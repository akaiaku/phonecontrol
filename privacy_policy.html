Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Call Filter Pro collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Contact Information

  • Contacts Data: We access your device's contact list to provide call blocking and contact management features
  • Call Logs: We read call history to identify blocked numbers and provide call statistics
  • Phone Numbers: We process phone numbers for blocking and identification purposes

1.2 Device Information

  • Device Permissions: We request permissions necessary for app functionality (contacts, phone, notifications)
  • App Usage: We may collect information about how you use the app to improve functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Call Blocking: Identifying and blocking unwanted calls based on your preferences
  • Contact Management: Organizing and managing your contact list
  • App Functionality: Providing core features like call logs, backup, and sync
  • Notifications: Sending relevant notifications about blocked calls and app updates

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your contacts, call logs, or personal information to external servers.

3.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure, app-specific storage areas
  • Access Control: Only the app can access its stored data

3.2 Backup and Sync

  • Backup files are encrypted and stored locally or in your chosen cloud storage
  • You have full control over backup creation and restoration
  • No data is transmitted without your explicit consent

4. Permissions Explained

4.1 Required Permissions

  • Contacts: To read and manage your contact list for blocking and organization
  • Phone: To access call logs and implement call blocking functionality
  • Storage: To save app data, settings, and backup files
  • Notifications: To notify you about blocked calls and app status

4.2 Optional Permissions

  • Microphone: Only if you enable voice-related features (currently not implemented)
  • Camera: For scanning QR codes or taking photos for contacts (if implemented)

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Your contacts, call logs, and personal information remain private and are not transmitted to external services.

5.1 No Third-Party Services

  • We do not use analytics services that collect personal data
  • We do not integrate with social media platforms
  • We do not sell or rent your information to advertisers

6. Your Rights and Controls

6.1 Data Control

  • Access: You can view all data stored by the app through the app's interface
  • Export: You can export your data using the backup feature
  • Delete: You can delete individual entries or clear all app data
  • Modify: You can edit contacts, call blocks, and settings at any time

6.2 Permission Management

  • You can revoke permissions at any time through device settings
  • The app will guide you to enable necessary permissions
  • You can use the app with limited functionality if some permissions are denied

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Notification of Changes: We will notify users of significant changes through the app or app store updates.

9. Open Source and Transparency

Call Filter Pro is committed to transparency:

  • Our app's source code practices data handling responsibly
  • You can verify our privacy claims through code inspection
  • We follow industry best practices for mobile app privacy

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • App Support: Through the app's settings page
  • Email: linhhvtt@gmail.com
  • Issue Reporting: Through the app's settings page or email

We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
